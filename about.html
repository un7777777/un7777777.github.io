About

RiskTrix is being shaped as a simple, credible way to present risk heatmaps.

The project is focused on helping teams communicate likelihood and impact clearly without adding unnecessary complexity or placeholder promises.

What guides the product

RiskTrix exists to make risk discussions easier to follow. The goal is a clear matrix presentation that supports better conversations between delivery teams, leadership, and clients.

The current website keeps that message narrow on purpose. It avoids listing speculative features and instead explains the product through its practical purpose: a risk heatmap tool that helps people read the situation quickly.

What matters on launch

  • Accuracy: public-facing copy should match the current build and approved client messaging.
  • Clarity: every section should explain RiskTrix in plain language without filler.
  • Readiness: the site should be clean enough to publish before the next sprint review.
