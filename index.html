What we are building

RiskTrix turns complex risk data into clear, decision-ready heatmaps.

Many teams struggle to communicate risk clearly. Spreadsheets and static reports often make it difficult to understand which risks matter most and where attention is needed.

RiskTrix solves this by transforming likelihood and impact into a structured, visual heatmap. This allows teams to quickly identify high-priority risks, align on decisions, and communicate insights effectively to stakeholders.

Rather than adding more complexity, we focus on clarity — making risk discussions faster, more consistent, and easier to act on.

Where RiskTrix fits

Designed for real decision moments, not just reporting

RiskTrix is built for the moments when teams need to explain risk clearly — in project reviews, stakeholder meetings, and executive discussions. Instead of relying on dense reports, teams can use a visual matrix to quickly communicate where attention is needed.

During discussions, the heatmap acts as a shared reference point. It helps align different stakeholders, reduces ambiguity, and keeps conversations focused on priority areas rather than raw data.

By turning risk into something visual and structured, RiskTrix supports faster, more confident decisions when it matters most.

How it works

Structured risk, not scattered data

RiskTrix organises risk using a standardised likelihood and impact matrix. Each risk is positioned within a fixed structure, ensuring consistency across teams and projects.

The 5×5 grid and colour gradient provide a clear visual hierarchy, allowing users to quickly distinguish between low, medium, and high-risk areas without additional explanation.

By applying a consistent structure to all risk data, RiskTrix makes comparisons easier and supports more reliable prioritisation across different contexts.
